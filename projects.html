Projects & Impact

My work spans cybersecurity policy, critical information infrastructure protection, compliance automation, digital transformation, and emerging technology research. Across government, academia, and public engagement, I have contributed to national cybersecurity legislation, critical infrastructure protection frameworks, stakeholder workshops, cyber capacity building, and research on AI, blockchain, privacy-preserving verification, and automated compliance.

Research, AI, Blockchain & Compliance Automation

SmartComply: NIST 800-53 Compliance Automation

An AI-driven compliance automation project that combines policy-to-control mapping, LLM-assisted analysis, and smart contract enforcement logic for critical infrastructure compliance workflows.

Trust-Weighted Compliance Assessment

A ledger-enforced trust model that derives participant trust from protocol-verifiable events such as participation, evidence quality, consistency, truthfulness, and assessment history across compliance cycles.

Privacy-Preserving Compliance Verification

A research direction focused on proving compliance claims without exposing sensitive vendor data, using off-chain evidence, on-chain commitments, proof receipts, and zero-knowledge verification.

Hybrid AI/ML Vulnerability-to-Control Mapping

A multi-method system that links cybersecurity weaknesses and vulnerabilities to NIST controls using LLM analysis, statistical similarity, rule-based matching, and confidence scoring.

AI, Blockchain & Cybersecurity Compliance Research

My current Ph.D. research explores secure, privacy-preserving, and automated cybersecurity policy compliance for Critical Information Infrastructure using AI, smart contracts, Hyperledger Fabric, and privacy-preserving verification.

GemSoft: Big Data and Database Integration

Developed a generic and open-source web application for integrating Big Data Platforms with traditional relational database management systems. The system supports data movement between platforms, graphical visualization, and database connectivity.
