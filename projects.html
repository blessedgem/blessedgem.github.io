Projects & Impact

My work spans cybersecurity policy, critical information infrastructure protection, compliance automation, digital transformation, and emerging technology research. Across government, academia, and public engagement, I have led and advanced work in national cybersecurity legislation, critical infrastructure protection frameworks, stakeholder engagement, cyber capacity building, and research on AI, blockchain, privacy-preserving verification, and automated compliance.

Research, AI, Blockchain & Compliance Automation

SmartComply: NIST 800-53 Compliance Automation

Designed an AI-driven compliance automation project that combines policy-to-control mapping, LLM-assisted analysis, and smart contract enforcement logic for critical infrastructure compliance workflows.

Trust-Weighted Compliance Assessment

Developed a ledger-enforced trust model that derives participant trust from protocol-verifiable events such as participation, evidence quality, consistency, truthfulness, and assessment history across compliance cycles.

Privacy-Preserving Compliance Verification

Advanced a research direction focused on proving compliance claims without exposing sensitive vendor data, using off-chain evidence, on-chain commitments, proof receipts, and zero-knowledge verification.

Hybrid AI/ML Vulnerability-to-Control Mapping

Developed a multi-method system that links cybersecurity weaknesses and vulnerabilities to NIST controls using LLM analysis, statistical similarity, rule-based matching, and confidence scoring.

AI, Blockchain & Cybersecurity Compliance Research

Lead Ph.D. research on secure, privacy-preserving, and automated cybersecurity policy compliance for Critical Information Infrastructure using AI, smart contracts, Hyperledger Fabric, and privacy-preserving verification.

GemSoft: Big Data and Database Integration

Developed a generic and open-source web application for integrating Big Data platforms with traditional relational database management systems. The system supports data movement between platforms, graphical visualization, and database connectivity.

Cybersecurity Education and Curriculum Development

Advanced cybersecurity curriculum development and academic support activities spanning secure systems, cybersecurity governance, risk management, technical controls, and applied computing practice.
